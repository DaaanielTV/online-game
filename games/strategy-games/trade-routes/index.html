Trade Empire Management

How to Play

  • Click on a city to view its resources and prices
  • Click another city to establish a trade route
  • Trade routes cost money based on distance
  • End your turn to collect profits from trade
  • Watch for price fluctuations and demand changes
  • Build your reputation through profitable trades

Cities & Resources

  • Each city produces unique resources
  • Prices vary based on local supply and demand
  • Trading partners affect city prosperity
  • Route efficiency may degrade over time
