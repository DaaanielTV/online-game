Space Colony

Module Types

Habitat - Houses colonists
Oxygen Generator - Produces oxygen
Power Plant - Generates power
Hydroponics - Produces food
Science Lab - Research points
Mining Drill - Extracts minerals

How to Play

  • Click a module type to select it
  • Click anywhere on the map to place the module
  • Keep your colonists alive by maintaining:
  • - Oxygen levels (Oxygen Generators)
  • - Power supply (Power Plants)
  • - Food production (Hydroponics)
  • Use Mining Drills to gather minerals for construction
  • Build Science Labs to research new technologies
  • Monitor alerts for resource shortages
  • Manage module efficiency based on power and oxygen levels
