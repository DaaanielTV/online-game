City Planner

How to Build Your City

  • Select a building type from the controls above
  • Choose the building size (1x1 to 3x3)
  • Click on the grid to place your building
  • Balance resources and keep your citizens happy
  • Monitor power and water consumption
  • Control pollution levels with parks and smart planning
  • Ensure enough jobs for your growing population

Residential

Houses your population

Commercial

Provides jobs and income

Industrial

Higher income but creates pollution

Park

Increases happiness, reduces pollution

Power Plant

Provides electricity for your city

Water Treatment

Supplies water to your citizens
