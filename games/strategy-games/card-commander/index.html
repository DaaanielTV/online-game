Card Commander

How to Play

  • Drag cards from your hand to the play area to deploy them
  • Each card costs energy to play - manage your energy wisely
  • Unit cards provide combat power
  • Strategy cards provide special effects
  • Your energy increases each turn
  • Draw a new card at the start of each turn
  • Build powerful combinations between different card types

Unit Cards

Basic combat units with power and special abilities

Commander Cards

Powerful units that enhance your other cards

Strategy Cards

Special effects that can turn the tide of battle
